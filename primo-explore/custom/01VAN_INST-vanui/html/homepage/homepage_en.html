What am I searching?
  • Everything: Most of the resources available from the Vanderbilt Libraries. This default search combines the Library Catalog and the Articles options.
  • Library Catalog: Everything in our physical and electronic collections (books, e-books, journals and periodicals, media, music, and more).
  • Articles: Full text articles, e-book chapters, and other content available through the Vanderbilt Libraries.
  • Course Reserves: Everything currently on reserve for Vanderbilt courses.
  • Electronic Resources: Ebooks, articles, streaming media, online scores. No physical (print) materials.
